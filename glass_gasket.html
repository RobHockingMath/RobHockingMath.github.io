Product Description

The Apollonian gasket originates from Apollonius of Perga (c. 200 BCE), who studied the problem of constructing a circle tangent to three given circles.

One 3D generalization is called the Apollonian sphere packing, and was first described in the 1930s by Frederick Soddy. However, as the sphere packing completely fills a sphere with smaller spheres, it is not very interesting to look at.
