Title



I've been aware of Menger sponges and their relatives since university. However, I never gave them much thought until I stumbled upon the following video by George Hart on their cross-sections:



This was in the spring of 2022. The first thought was to 3D print the sponge in two halves in such such a way that there was a mechanism for locking the halves together and then pulling them apart. I iterated on a few designs before eventually settling on this one - a steel 3D print with inlaid rare earth magnets.
